Jasmine Milk Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Loose leaf jasmine green tea
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Dried Jasmine Flowers

Steps

  1. Add jasmine green tea leaves to the teapot
  2. Boil water and let it cool to 180F degrees
  3. Pour the water over the tea leaves and leave to steep for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add honey (or sweetener) to a heat-safe jar.
  5. Pour the tea into the jar and give it a stir to dissolve the honey.
  6. Add 1 cup of milk to the tea and stir.
  7. Add 1 tablespoon of dried jasmine flowers to the tea, give it another stir and let cool.
  8. Cover with a lid and place in the fridge to cool overnight.
